Concentration is heavy: the top 20 vendors account for 75% of spend and Salesforce alone is 40%. The opportunities below are ordered by financial impact. M&A transaction advisors ($313K) and statutory spend ($26K) are in the total but excluded from rationalization as one-time or non-discretionary.
