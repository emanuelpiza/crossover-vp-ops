# Vendor Spend Review: $770K–$1.16M in annual savings (10–15%)

Following the acquisition, we reviewed 386 active vendors totaling $7.84M in trailing-12-month spend and identified $770K–$1.16M in savings (10–15%).

Concentration is heavy: the top 20 vendors account for 75% of spend and Salesforce alone is 40%. The opportunities below are ordered by financial impact. M&A transaction advisors ($313K) and statutory spend ($26K) are in the total but excluded from rationalization as one-time or non-discretionary.

## DECISION REQUESTED

- Authorize the **Salesforce seat audit** and confirm whether Salesforce is strategically protected ($467–779K).
- Authorize the **lease break-clause + headcount audit** across the 6 offices ($144–230K combined).
- Approve **HubSpot + Kimble termination** ($64K combined, low risk).

## SAVINGS OPPORTUNITIES

### CRM + PSA stack cleanup (HubSpot + Kimble) — $64K — CRO

HubSpot ($32K) is a redundant second CRM running in parallel with Salesforce. Kimble PSA ($53K) can be absorbed into Salesforce-native functionality or the parent's existing PSA stack. Confirm no active HubSpot workflows before termination.
